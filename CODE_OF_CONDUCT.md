# Code of Conduct

MarketLab contributors are expected to communicate respectfully and keep collaboration professional.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, factual, and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, docs, and decisions rather than people.
- Keep review comments specific and actionable.
- Respect differing experience levels and work styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or review.
- Personal attacks or hostile language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove abusive content, request behavior changes, or revoke contribution access if needed to protect the project.
